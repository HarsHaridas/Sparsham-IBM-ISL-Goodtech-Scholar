About Us

Sparsham is the charity club of mace which enables the humanity in students,helps the future.

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering pioneered engineering education in central Kerala in 1961. Managed by Mar Athanasius College Association, Aided by the Government of Kerala, the college was first in Asia under Christian Management.

It was also the first engineering college to get affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi University and now affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. MACE, as it is popularly known, has grown by leaps and bounds since. It now has six full fledged departments apart from the two auxiliary departments of Mathematics and Humanities and Science.
